As the **Founding iOS Engineer** at **Second Nature Computing, Inc.** (April 2026 – Present), I'm building **Poppy**, a B2C AI-assistant app, from the ground up.

## What I do

- **0 to 1 product ownership:** Architecting the native iOS app end-to-end, from the SwiftUI interface to the on-device and cloud AI plumbing that powers the assistant.
- **AI-first experiences:** Designing conversational and assistive flows that make a large-language-model-backed assistant feel fast, reliable, and genuinely useful in everyday consumer scenarios.
- **Foundational engineering:** Standing up the app's core infrastructure (state management, sync, networking, and observability) so the product can scale as the team and feature set grow.

## Why it matters

Poppy is a chance to define a consumer AI product at its earliest stage. As the founding engineer, I'm responsible for the technical foundation the whole app is built on: the architecture decisions, the developer velocity, and the bar for quality and polish that a B2C audience expects.

## Tech Stack

Swift, SwiftUI, iOS, large language models, REST/streaming APIs.
