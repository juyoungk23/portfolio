Hello, I'm Juyoung Kim, a **Software Engineer** and **Product Builder** based in San Francisco.

I like working at both ends of the stack: the backend systems that have to be right, and the user experience that has to feel right. Currently I'm the Founding iOS Engineer at **Second Nature Computing**, building **Poppy**, a proactive AI assistant. Before that I spent three years on data infrastructure at **Wells Fargo**. On my own time I've shipped immersive 3D/XR products for the Apple Vision Pro and [TAPE](/posts/tape-mma-fight-intelligence), a computer-vision system that turns MMA broadcasts into verified fight analytics.

## What I do

- **Ship end-to-end products:** I take ideas from 0 to 1, from database architecture (Supabase/Postgres) to native UI (SwiftUI/React/Next.js).
- **Build immersive tech:** Gaussian splats, VR180 video, and shared spatial experiences on visionOS, usually in territory where there's no reference implementation to copy.
- **Design reliable systems:** I bring the habits of banking infrastructure (observability, validation, graceful failure) to products that ship fast.

## Why I like this work

My favorite projects need both creative prototyping and systems engineering: a sync engine that makes watching a fight with a friend in VR feel effortless, or a data pipeline honest enough that a coach trusts its numbers. First it has to work. Then it should feel like magic.
