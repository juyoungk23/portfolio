I am a **Software / Product Engineer** based in San Francisco. I want software that feels like magic and doesn't fall over.

I spent three years on data pipelines at **Wells Fargo**, where the job was zero data loss at enterprise scale. Then I went to the other extreme: immersive products for **Apple Vision Pro**, a solo computer-vision system for MMA analytics, and now a consumer AI assistant as the **Founding iOS Engineer** at **Second Nature Computing**.

I am currently looking for a Founding Engineer or Product Engineer role at a high-growth startup where I can own the full stack, from the database to the UI.

## Selected Work

### [TAPE](/posts/tape-mma-fight-intelligence)
**The deep dive.**
A solo-built system that turns ordinary MMA broadcast footage into verified fight analytics: a five-layer computer vision pipeline, a human review tool, and a live product site where every stat links back to the tape.

### [Poppy](/posts/poppy-second-nature)
**The day job.**
As Founding iOS Engineer at Second Nature Computing, I build Poppy, a proactive AI assistant that reads your calendar, email, messages, and health data, then flags what matters before you ask.

### [SHARP Memories](/posts/sharp-memories)
**The nostalgia product.**
A cross-platform app that turns a single 2D photo into a 3D Gaussian Splat. An event-driven serverless pipeline (Supabase + Modal) delivers the 3D asset to an iOS App Clip, so recipients can view it without installing anything.

### [Immersive Watch Party SDK](/posts/immersive-watch-party)
**The SDK.**
VR is isolating, so I fixed it. I built an SDK that syncs `AVPlayer` instances across devices alongside Spatial Personas and packaged it as a drop-in binary framework. Apple still hasn't shipped this for the Vision Pro.

### [Vantage](/posts/vantage-vr-sports-platform)
**The passion project.**
I'm a huge UFC fan, so I built a combat sports streaming app for spatial computing. Watching MMA on Vantage feels like sitting in the arena, and with the Watch Party SDK you're sitting there with friends.

### [Wells Fargo Infrastructure](/posts/wells-fargo-data-engineering)
**The foundation.**
Three years migrating legacy ETL pipelines to Google Cloud. That's where observability, testing, and graceful failure handling got drilled into me.
