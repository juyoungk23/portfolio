I built an Immersive Watch Party feature that makes fans feel like they're in the arena together.

## What it does

- Blends Apple's Spatial Persona with SharePlay and immersive video for a shared VR experience.
- Lets friends co-watch events with presence, not just video playback.
- First of its kind for this use case.

## Skills

iOS, visionOS, Unity, C#, SwiftUI, SharePlay, Spatial Persona, Immersive Video, Full-Stack Development, Analytics.
