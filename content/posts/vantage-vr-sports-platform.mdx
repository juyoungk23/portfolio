Vantage lets fans watch sports stereoscopically and feel like they're in the arena, shipping on both Apple Vision Pro and Meta Quest.

## What I built

- Full-stack, cross-platform app for Vision Pro and Quest.
- Analytics, payments, and distribution for real, paying users (hundreds of users, about $20/month revenue so far).
- Production videography direction for three professional combat sports events.

## Skills

Apple Vision Pro, Meta Quest, Unity, C#, SwiftUI, SharePlay, Spatial Persona, Immersive Video, Full-Stack Development, Analytics.
